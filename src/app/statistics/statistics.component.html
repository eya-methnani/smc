Statistics Showcase

After conducting a survey, we have concluded that there are several important factors that influence school results. These include mental health, stress, anxiety, mood, self-esteem, physical health, exercise frequency, eating habits, social life, group activities, and comfort in school.

Data Insights

School Results Distribution

The pie chart highlights an imbalanced distribution in school results, prompting the use of random oversampling to improve accuracy.

Mental Health Distribution

The histogram shows a distribution of mental well-being ratings. The distribution appears more balanced, with a slight skew towards higher ratings. This suggests that, on average, respondents report positive mental well-being. However, we still have some respondents who reported lower mental health ratings.

Mental Health Outliers

The boxplot helps identify outliers in our dataset, which will be addressed before model training.
